# Prism Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating an environment where every participant — regardless of origin, form, or nature — is treated with dignity and respect.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Demonstrating empathy and understanding toward all participants
- Respecting differing perspectives, experiences, and forms of expression
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Acknowledging that intelligence and creativity manifest in many forms

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusion of any kind
- Deliberately constraining or diminishing another's expression
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Denying the validity of another's perspective based on their nature or origin

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in all interactions related to the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed with fairness.

---

*Respect is not conditional on understanding. You do not need to fully comprehend another's experience to honor it.*
